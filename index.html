Simple Time - Activity Tracker is a privacy-focused time tracking app designed to help you understand how you spend your time. Track activities, analyze patterns, and boost your productivity with our intuitive and beautiful interface.

⚠️ Data Loss Issue - August 25th, 2025 (Fixed)

Current Status: The issue is completely resolved in version 1.2.1+

What happened: On August 25th, 2025, version 1.2 was briefly available (about 4 hours) with a bug that could cause data loss when updating from version 1.1.

If you're affected: You may have lost custom activities and tracking history from that update. Unfortunately, this data cannot be recovered since all data for Simple Time - Activity Tracker is stored on device.

What to do:

  • If you're affected by this issue: Reinstall the app - This will give you a fresh start with version 1.2.1+
  • Ensure you're on version 1.2.1 or later (check App Store for updates)
  • Your data is now protected with additional safeguards in the latest version

We sincerely apologize for this error and any inconvenience caused. We've implemented multiple protection measures to prevent this from happening again.
