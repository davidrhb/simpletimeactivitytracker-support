Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Simple Time - Activity Tracker does not collect or transmit any personal data. All information is stored locally on your device.

Data Types Stored

The app stores your time tracking data including activity names, start/end times, and duration. This data is used solely to provide the app's functionality and is never transmitted outside of your device.

Local Storage

Your data is stored securely on your device using iOS's built-in SwiftData framework, which uses local storage.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your time tracking data may be synced across your devices through Apple's iCloud service. This is handled entirely by Apple and follows Apple's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it within the app or delete the app entirely. No data is stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Simple Time - Activity Tracker does not use any third-party analytics services. For in-app purchases, the app uses Apple's StoreKit framework. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple, not by Simple Time - Activity Tracker.

App Store & Purchases

When making in-app purchases, standard App Store policies apply. Your purchase information is handled according to Apple's privacy policy, not by Simple Time - Activity Tracker directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store.

Effective Date

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025.
